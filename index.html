Features

Cubyz is a limitless indie game which uses voxels as a base, but don't think that's all! We have a ton of our own features. If you want your own features, you can expand the game with addons.

TL;DR: Like a similar cube game, but with more possibilities.

- No height limit
- No depth limit
- Farther render distances
- Immersive fog of all colors
- Crystals of all colors
- Colored glass that filters light
- Mod and addon friendly
